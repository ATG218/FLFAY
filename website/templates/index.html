Mission Statement

Financial Literacy For All Youth is a social impact initiative with the goal of preparing the next generation with financial literacy. Many historically marginalized communities may not have the access to resources and often fall into a cycle of bad habits. We promote and offer avenues for everyone to learn how to be smarter with their money. We also work to fundraise to support other organizations who share our mission.
